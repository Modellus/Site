Interactive Simulations

Modellus brings abstract concepts to life, allowing users to manipulate and observe phenomena in real-time, fostering a deeper understanding of scientific principles.

  • Easily move and interact with elements within your simulations to explore various scenarios and their outcomes.
  • Work with realistic visual models that help bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and the real world.
  • Modify variables and observe immediate effects, enabling hands-on experimentation and analysis.

Experiment Measurement

Beyond just simulating, Modellus empowers you to integrate real-world data and precise measurements into your educational content, making learning more authentic and analytical.

  • Incorporate video footage into your projects, allowing for in-depth analysis of real-world phenomena.
  • Precisely measure distances, angles, and other quantities directly within your digital environment.
  • Organize and analyze experimental data efficiently, facilitating clear data representation and interpretation.

Natural Math Writing

  • Input mathematical equations using a natural, intuitive interface that mirrors traditional handwriting.
  • Equations and mathematical models written within Modellus seamlessly link to and drive your simulations, providing instant visual feedback.
  • Utilize an unbounded digital workspace to organize your thoughts, equations, and simulations without spatial constraints.

Modellus simplifies the integration of mathematics into your projects, allowing for intuitive input and seamless connection between equations and simulations on an expansive canvas.

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Our FAQ covers the most common topics about using Modellus — from understanding subscription plans and marketplace credits to sharing models and working with students. Whether you're a new user, an educator, or just exploring your options, you'll find clear answers to help you make the most of the platform.

What’s included in each subscription plan?

Each plan offers different levels of access. Free Plan: Up to 3 models, access to free marketplace items, and model sharing. Standard Plan (€10/month): Unlimited models, €5 marketplace credit, and early access to new features. Premium Plan (€20/month): All of the above, plus €15 marketplace credit and an AI assistant to help create models.

What can I find in the marketplace?

The marketplace offers pre-built models, assets, and educational tools created by the community and Modellus team. Items may be free or paid and help speed up model creation or enhance teaching and learning.

How does the monthly marketplace credit work?

Standard and Premium plans receive monthly credits (€5 or €15) that can be used to purchase items from the marketplace. Credits carry over to the next month.

Can I share my models with others?

Yes! All plans allow you to share your models with other users. This is especially useful for teachers, collaborators, or teams working on similar topics.

I’m a teacher. Can I use Modellus with my students?

Absolutely. Modellus is ideal for classroom use. Teachers can create models and share them with students, assign work, and use the platform to enhance STEM education through simulation and visualization.

What is the AI assistant, and how can I use it?

Available in the Premium plan, the AI assistant helps users create models more quickly by offering suggestions, automating repetitive tasks, and assisting with model logic and structure.
